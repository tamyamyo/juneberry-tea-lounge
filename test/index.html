03 Our Story

A neighborhood lounge, built around the cup.

Juneberry began as a quiet idea: that a great cup of tea deserves a place worthy of it. Not a counter to grab and go from, but a lounge to settle into.

We named her after the juneberry tree — modest, generous, easy to overlook until you taste what it offers. That's the spirit we wanted in a tea lounge. Refined, but never precious. Considered, but always warm.

"The best teas deserve the best setting — a place where craft meets comfort, and slowing down feels natural."

— Juneberry
